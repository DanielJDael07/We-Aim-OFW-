In the heart of the Philippines, where the spirit of community and resilience thrives, a remarkable NGO has emerged with a mission to make a lasting impact on the lives of Filipinos both at home and abroad. WE AIM OFW, as its name suggests, is an organization with a clear vision and a profound mission, dedicated to uplifting the welfare of Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) and their families.

WE AIM OFW is a shining example of what can be achieved when passionate individuals unite under a shared vision. By fostering collaboration, establishing OMG Communities, and focusing on family shelter, livelihood, and education, this NGO is actively making a tangible difference in the lives of Overseas Filipino Workers and their families. As they continue their tireless efforts, they embody the spirit of unity and hope, working toward a brighter future for all Filipinos, whether they are at home or abroad.

Vision

To be one of the active NGOs that will greatly working hand in hand with the Philippine Government or any other entities or different organizations in caring and catering Overall Filipino Welfare here and abroad.

Mission

To establish a One Model & Great (OMG) Community project in every City or Municipality that will care in every City or Municipality that will care ad cater the Overall Filipino Welfare. In terms of their Family Shelter, Source of Livelihood & Education Scholarship for their loving children.

Goal

To create a well being community that will surely cater and care each and every family in their areas. With a satisfying daily living and with a great future ahead of each of them.

Purpose

To have a ONE MODEL & GREAT community that will sustain and maintain the needs of every Filipino Family. For the betterment of each of and definitely not a burden to society.
